## Purpose

`Archive/` is the safe boundary for old, imported, replaced, broken, or historical material that should stay accessible without polluting active standards.

## Archive boundary rule

```text
Active Systems ─────────► documented, validated, current naming
      │
      └────► Archive/ ───► preserved, reference-only, not authoritative
```

## Archive usage rules
- do not treat archived naming or structure as the active standard
- use archive content as migration input, audit material, or source reference
- mark replacements clearly when active systems supersede archive systems
